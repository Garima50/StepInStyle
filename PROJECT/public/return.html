Return and Refund Policy

Last updated: August 31, 2024

Returns

All returns must be postmarked within 30 days of the purchase date. All returned items must be in new and unused condition, with all original tags and labels attached. There are some conditions for returning the product:

  • Items must be in new and unused condition.

  • Original tags and labels must be attached.

  • Return must be postmarked within 30 days of purchase.

Return Process

To return an item, please email customer service at "StepInStyle@gmail.com" to obtain a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number. This will allow you to return the item correctly and will help us process your return quickly. Further, Pack the item securely in its original packaging. Include the RMA number on the package, then ship the item back to the address provided by the customer service.

Refunds

Refunds will be issued in the original form of payment within 5-7 business days of receiving the returned item. They will only be issued to the original payment method. Returns are subject to a restocking fee.

FAQ's

  • How do I return a defective or faulty product?If your item is potentially defective or faulty, and it's been less than 30 days since your purchase, simply return the item. If it's been more than 30 days since your purchase, please call us to return the item.

  • Can I exchange my order?The best way to exchange an order is to simply place a new order for the item you want and return the item you already have.

  • What sizes do you offer?We offer a wide range of sizes for men, women, and children. You can find the size chart on each product page to help you choose the right fit.

  • What if my return is outside the 30-day window?Returns outside the 30-day window are not eligible for a refund or exchange. However, if there are extenuating circumstances, please contact our customer service team for assistance.

  • How do I track my return?Once you've shipped your return, you can track it using the tracking number provided by your chosen shipping service. We'll also notify you by email once we've received and processed your return.

  • What happens if I lose my return package? If you lose your return package, please contact our customer service team immediately. We will work with you to determine the best course of action.

  • What if my return is denied? If your return is denied, you will be notified by email. You will have the option to re-ship the item to you or have it donated to charity.

  • What if my item is damaged during shipping?If your item is damaged during shipping, please contact our customer service team immediately. We will work with you to resolve the issue.
